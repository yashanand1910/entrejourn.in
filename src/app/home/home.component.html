Our Mission: Raise Awareness

EntreJourn is an innovative and the first-of-its-kind community-based project that welcomes entrepreneurs from every discipline and culture who are not just passionate about their venture but are also willing to share their entrepreneurial journey, their struggles and challenges with respect to mental well-being. With these narratives, we hope to engage entrepreneurs to share and also spark conversations around mental health amongst entrepreneurs.
